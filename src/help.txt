Start Item
<s(item#)0001>
stop
<s(item#)0000>

change item time
<t(item#)00(time value, takes 3 chars, hex)>

Add item
<i000(value, 3 chars, not hex)>

add color
<a(item#)00(value, 3 chars, hex)>

Change position (index command)
<p(item#)00(value, 3 chars, hex)>
ex: <p20000F>

Change direction
<d(item#)0000(1 or 0)>
ex: <d200001>
or <d200000>

Change color length
<l(item#)(color#)0(value, 3 chars, hex)>
